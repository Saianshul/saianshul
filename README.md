## About Me

I am a student at UC San Diego pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science. I have a strong passion for building software that is efficient, scalable, user-friendly, and most importantly, impactful.

As a creative and perseverant problem solver, I am dedicated to using technology to build tangible solutions for real-world challenges. Utilizing the skills that I have developed from projects and courses, I am eager to dive deep into a problem's core to engineer resilient software that meets real-world demands and delivers lasting value.

I am proficient in languages such as Python, JavaScript, and Java, and I have a mastery of core CS skills such as data structures and algorithms and object-oriented programming/design.
